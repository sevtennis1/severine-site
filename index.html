Séverine Toussaert

Associate Professor of Economics, University of Oxford

Tutorial Fellow in Economics and Fellow for Research, St John's College
Affiliations: BR-UK Research Network, CEPR, CESifo
Board Member, Review of Economic Studies

I'm a behavioral economist interested in understanding how people make decisions--especially when those decisions involve temptation, self-control, and uncertainty. My research spans behavioral decision theory, public health applications (particularly smoking cessation), and metascience. I'm passionate about research transparency and enjoy exploring the "behind the scenes" of academic work.
