Podcast & Media

I'm passionate about sharing the hidden side of research--the trials, errors, and lessons that usually stay behind the scenes. Here are my ongoing projects exploring the real stories behind academic work.

🎧

Behavioural Science Uncovered

Each episode covers the real story behind one of the speaker's papers. How did they start this project? What hurdles did they encounter? How long did it really take? What would they have done differently?

Listen at bsuncovered.com →
🎙️

Behind-the-Scenes Seminar Series

A new online seminar format: a traditional short presentation from the speaker, followed by live forecasting from the audience about elements of the research journey. The speaker then covers each topic and debriefs.

Watch at bts-seminar.net →

At Oxford

Activities I run or take part in at Oxford, around academic community and wellbeing.

🏛️

James Maynard, Fields Medalist: Behind the Scenes

An in-person Behind-the-Scenes Seminar at St John's College, Oxford — the same format as the online series but hosted live.

💡

Failing Well

Events exploring how academics navigate setbacks, rejection, and failure — and what it means to fail well in a research career.

Mentoring

One-on-one and group mentoring for students and early-career researchers at Oxford.
